About IUSCM

The Institute of Universal Self Consciousness Movement ("IUSCM") has been established by Dr.Satyanarayana Chillapa ("Swamiji") to propagate the Oneness of mankind globally. This is to be achieved through the emergence of Universal Self Consciousness in every individual.

Oneness of mankind is not merely an intellectual understanding; it is organically integral to one's Consciousness. It involves empirical experience of oneself reflected in all beings, and all beings reflected in oneself much like how a father biologically feels reflected in all his children.

The message of IUSCM empirically demonstrates that the entire universe and its variety are but mental imagery; the unmanifested, homogeneous true Self is the only source and the underlying common phenomenon, referred to as the "Universal I" or the "Supreme Being."

About Dr. Satyanarayana Chillapa (Swamiji)

Swamiji was a senior Professor and University Head of the Department, Extension Education, Andhra Pradesh Agricultural University, Hyderabad, India.

He came from a traditional Indian family devoted to Vedic learning and culture, with a deep attachment to the Advaitic teachings of Bhagavatpada Sri Adi Sankara.

He was blessed by his Master, Sri Samartha Sadguru Babuji Maharaj, with the grace of "Aja Smruti," a revelatory consciousness of the corporeally unborn state, considered the highest attainment in Yoga culture.

Swamiji attained Mahasamadhi in 2025, leaving behind a rich spiritual legacy that continues to inspire practitioners worldwide.
